Built for the people who deliver care

Share careTalk with managers and support workers who need practical help in the moment — not another heavy clinical system.

For support workers

  • For support workers Say “Hi careTalk” and describe what’s happening. Get do / don’t reminders, then answer one documentation question at a time.
  • Voice notes & live reports Capture what mattered on shift and build a report as you speak — ready for handover or the agency.

For managers and nurses

  • For managers, nurses & supervisors Review reports by category, approve registrations, and teach careTalk your home’s local practice.
  • Train from trusted sources Pull guidance from UK care sources, or paste a policy URL, so answers stay closer to how your service works.

How a home can try it

No install required for the web demo. Use Chrome or Edge and allow the microphone when prompted.

  1. Open the demo

    Start on Sign in / Register. Use the test admin if you only want a quick look.

  2. Talk through a scenario

    Try a fall, swallow concern, or wellbeing note — careTalk guides the next safe step and the write-up.

  3. Review as a manager

    Open Reports and Give careTalk more knowledge to see how oversight and local training fit together.

Data stays on the device (browser storage) unless you configure agency email or webhook forwarding. careTalk supports documentation and reminders — it does not replace clinical assessment, your policies, or calling 999 in an emergency.
