  • Voice notes & live reports Build a report as you speak — ready for handover, the agency, or live supervisor webhooks.
  • For managers, nurses & supervisors Review reports by category, approve registrations, and watch live webhook updates as carers create records on site.
  • Train from your practice Teach careTalk custom care training from UK sources or a policy URL so documentation matches how your service works.

How a home can try it

No install required for the web demo. Use Chrome or Edge and allow the microphone when prompted.

  1. Open the demo

    Start on Sign in / Register. Use the test admin if you only want a quick look.

  2. Talk through a scenario

    Try a fall, swallow concern, or wellbeing note — careTalk guides the next safe step and the write-up.

  3. Review as a manager

    Open Reports and Give careTalk more knowledge to see how oversight and local training fit together.

Data stays on the device (browser storage) unless you configure agency email or webhook forwarding. careTalk supports documentation and reminders — it does not replace clinical assessment, your policies, or calling 999 in an emergency.
